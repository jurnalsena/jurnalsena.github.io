What is Luna?
Luna is a visual novel engine, developed by Cyberus Studio,
made using JavaScript.

Where does it run?
Luna can runs well on Windows, Linux, and Mac OS.

What you need?
Text editor for script editing, we recommend you using Sublime Text or Notepad++.
You can use NWjs to make it a desktop application, and play it after you finish the project,
also you can upload your game to your hosting to play it online.
